About

Map built from the pre-EA Subnautica 2 (build 5.6.1-112084) PAK files via the included data miner. Coordinates are raw Unreal Engine cm presented in L.CRS.Simple; depth is computed from world Z.

Hillshade and biome tint are computed in your browser from the extracted heightmap and polygons (no pre-baked PNGs). Game content © Unknown Worlds Entertainment.
